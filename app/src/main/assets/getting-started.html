Welcome to BreakTime!

BreakTime is an app that helps you study in manageable chunks. Pick how long you'd like to study and how long you'd like your breaks to last, then flip your phone to start studying!

How to Use

If you want to get started right away, just press Start Studying, then flip your phone over. Your flash will blink to let you know that your timer is counting down.

Don't get distracted while you're studying! If you flip your phone over, your study session will pause.

Once your session is up, your phone will vibrate. Flip your phone face-up and pick how you'd like to spend your 5-minute break, then relax.

After your break is up, you can tap the Study button to start a new session, or you can snooze the alarm to take another 5 minutes. Don't procrastinate too long!

Changing Study and Break Periods

Want to study a bit less each session? Looking for a longer break? You can change the study or break periods in the Settings screen. Just tap the gear on any screen to get there.

Adding New Break Apps

If you want to use different apps during your break, you can add new apps in the Settings screen. Tap the gear on any screen to change your settings. Use the plus button to add a new app or the minus button to remove a suggested break app.
